Who Are We?

BEEDAYA is a passionate collective of architects, designers, and engineers, united by a common vision: to transform your aspirations into concrete, functional, and inspiring spaces. We are creators, technicians, and expert consultants, dedicated to excellence at every stage of your architectural, civil engineering, and real estate development projects.

Our Services

Complete Expertise from Consulting to Implementation

Strategic Consulting & Preliminary Studies

  • Architectural Consulting: Feasibility studies, potential analysis, conceptual sketches
  • Real Estate Development Consulting: Market analysis, program optimization, development strategies
  • Civil Engineering Consulting: Initial diagnostics, preliminary soil studies, structural technical consulting

Design & Engineering

  • Detailed Architectural Design (APS, APD, Execution Plans)

Construction Supervision
